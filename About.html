About Us

Nestled on the scenic slopes of the Aberdare Ranges and overlooking the splendid Mount Kenya, Teams Launch Pad Gataragwa is a unique outdoor retreat and learning center designed to ignite team synergy and personal growth.

Located just off the Nyeri–Nyahururu Highway on Bellevue Road, our center offers more than just training - it's an immersive experience in nature where teams connect, grow, and thrive. Set in a serene, natural environment, our workshops blend nature and learning, delivering results that resonate beyond the retreat. Our interactive and energizing programs are crafted to inspire collaboration, leadership, and innovation.

Whether you're building a stronger team, unlocking potential, or simply seeking a refreshing escape with purpose, The Teams Launch Pad Gataragwa is your destination for transformation.
